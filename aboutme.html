Amanda Barry is a graduate student from the University of Miami with a major in Marketing and minor in Psychology. A combination of her studies and passion for design has led her to pursue a graduate degree in Interactive Media. Her interests in design started in her early years with drawing and has grown in the Adobe Create Suite. In her college years, Amanda held multiple positions, including the Merchandise Designer for her Sorority and the Ad Designer at the Miami Hurricane Newspaper. She is driven to contribute her ideas and creativity to future endeavors.
